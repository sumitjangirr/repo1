Crops

KRISHIPHAL focuses on agroforestry and medicinal crops which are not labor intensive, are more efficiently farmed through mechanization, and can be stored for extended periods of time after harvest for minimized price volatility.

Agroforestry – Australian teak, Chandan…etc.

Medicinal Herbs – Safed Musli, Stevia, Kalmegh, Aswagandha, Coleus, Shatawar, Annato, Gurmar, Sarpgandha, Kaunch…etc.

Aromatic Plants – Citromila, Lemon grass, Pamarosa, Jamarosa, Buch, Patchonli, Tulsi, Geranium…etc.

Spices – Black pepper, Turmeric…etc.
