Company

Co-creating a business model for strong, dynamic agriculture that uses diverse sources of knowledge

Krishiphal is a Joint venture of Maa Dhanteshwaari herbal farms (MDHF) and FarmConnects, and is involved in providing agricultural advisory services for operation of agroforestry and organic herbal farms. Its advisory board is composed of eminent personalities from diverse backgrounds.

Landowners who do not personally farm their land can benefit greatly from the tailored, professional management service that Krishiphal delivers. We understand your complex concerns, your personal goals, and your investment aims, and we address each with a careful hand and an overall emphasis on soil stewardship, production, marketing, accounting, and communications.

The JV has access to more 2000 acres of land under cultivation across India and is actively engaged in providing end to end support to farmers on growing trees and herbal plants through organic methods, providing agronomy services and marketing of agricultural produce.

Brief of JV Partners

Maa Dhanteshwaari Herbals Group (MDHG)

Established in 1995 in Raipur, Bastar District, Chattisgarh under the leadership of Dr. Rajaram Tripathi is India’s first certified organic herbal farming company. It is engaged in farming, manufacturing and marketing of certified organic herbal, medicinal, aromatic plants, spices and essential oils.

MDHG caters to increasing demand of food, pharmaceutical, perfumery, flavor and cosmetic industry in India and international market. The groups’ pursuit of excellence has earned it national and international honors.

FarmConnects - is a farming company run by group of professionals with a passion for agriculture and environment. They have worked with leading companies in the BFSI sector and possess deep working understanding of retail, rural agro supply chains, real estate, training and business development. Presently they grow medicinal plants in Uttaranchal using latest technologies with a focus on creating efficiencies on yields and profitable farming.

Leadership and Advisory Board

Dr. Rajaram Tripathi, Founder

The contributions of Dr. Tripathi to Agroforestry and organic herbal farming in India and internationally cannot be overstated. Dr. Tripathi extensively researched on traditional healing practices followed by tribals in Bastar region of Chhattisgarh, India and single handedly improved the lives of thousands of farmers by promoting organic herbal farming. He is best known for research into and promotion of more than 30 medicinal herbs particularly focusing on Safed Musli and Stevia. Dr. Tripathi is a great agricultural educator, researcher and humanitarian, and has gained worldwide acclaim for his contributions to organic herbal farming.


Dr. Tripathi has been conferred with the prestigious Ayurveda Ratna by the Government of India.

Chander Chellani, Founder

Chander Chellani is a qualified Chartered Accountant with over 20 years’ experience in sales, marketing and general management functions in leading financial Services, insurance and consultancy companies. He has expertise in architecting and mentoring rapid ‘scale-up and growth” including turnarounds.


He has served successful stints with Citigroup, ICICI group and the Birla group and has been part of the executive committee and senior leadership teams. His business consulting includes engagements with several businesses in various sectors such as banking, insurance, consumer durables, micro finance institutions, agriculture, dairy and the service industry in India.

Vision

To be a global full service Farm Management Company, dedicated to provide “turnkey” services with end to end expertise in Agroforestry and plantation development for organic herbal, medicinal, aromatic plants, spices and essential oils.


To be a largest marketing organization of the above mentioned products.
